What are cryptocurrencies, a fashionable investment element

Public 30/05/2021

Cryptocurrencies are global, virtual and decentralized currencies that give full control to their users Although it seems like a new concept, cryptocurrencies have been in circulation for more than two decades Investing in cryptocurrencies is high risk but allows you to reap great benefits Cryptocurrencies, cryptocurrencies or crypto assets are global, virtual and decentralized currencies that allow you to acquire goods and services safely and without intermediaries. Being a non-centralized currency, they are not very susceptible to variables such as the economy or politics of a country. In addition, those who control them is the cryptocurrency user himself and not any financial or government entity. Although the concept of cryptocurrency seems relatively new, the truth is that this type of currency has been in circulation for more than two decades. In 1983 the first electronic monetary cryptographic system emerged, created by David Chaum, called ecash. Subsequently, DigiCash was implemented, which was used to carry out anonymous transactions but with a centralized system that required software that avoided its traceability. Later, in 2009, the true cryptocurrency boom began to originate after the creation of Bitcoin, the first digital currency.
